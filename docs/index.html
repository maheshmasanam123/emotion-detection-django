How it works

📷
1. Open camera

Allow browser camera access on the Live page. Your face is processed locally — no images leave your machine.

🧠
2. Detect emotion

A pretrained DeepFace model classifies your mood into angry / happy / neutral / sad in milliseconds.

🎶
3. Play a mood journey

Instead of just matching your mood, we play a curated journey that transitions you to a better one.

Features

📷
Live webcam detection
No file upload needed — point and detect.
🔗
YouTube / Spotify / SoundCloud
Embed external tracks alongside local MP3s.
🎵
Auto-advancing playlist
Prev / Next / Shuffle / Auto-advance controls.
👤
User accounts & history
Sign up, log in, browse past predictions.
🛠️
Django admin panel
Manage songs and view all predictions.
🪂
Graceful fallback
Works even when DeepFace can't run.
